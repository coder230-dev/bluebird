How to Play Solitaire

The goal is to move all cards to the four Foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.

  1. Build Tableau piles down by alternating colors and in descending order.
  2. Only a King can be placed on an empty Tableau spot.
  3. Draw cards from the Stock pile if no moves are available.
Good luck!
